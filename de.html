📚 Introduction to JetClub

The JetClub (Junior Engineers, Technicians, and Scientists Club) is a science and technology-focused extracurricular organization designed to nurture students' curiosity, creativity, and innovation. It provides a platform for learners to explore scientific principles, engineering concepts, and technological innovations in a practical and engaging way.

JetClub is more than a club — it is a movement that empowers young minds to think critically, solve real-world problems, and pursue careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields.

🎯 Aims and Objectives

The JetClub aims to:

💫
Foster students' interest in science, engineering, and technology
🔧
Provide practical experiences that complement classroom learning
💡
Promote creativity, innovation, and critical thinking
🤝
Encourage teamwork, leadership, and communication skills
🚀
Inspire students to pursue STEM-related careers
🌍
Engage members in solving real-world challenges
